FAQ

Good To Know.

The questions I hear most from my brides, answered honestly. If yours is not here, just message me on WhatsApp and ask.

Ask Me
Anything

No question is too small when it is about your wedding day.

01How far in advance should I book?

Bridal dates fill quickly, especially in wedding season, so lock your date as soon as it is finalized. Message me on WhatsApp and I will confirm availability right away.

02Is a trial required?

I strongly recommend one for bridal bookings. It is where we perfect your look together, well ahead of the big day, so the wedding morning holds zero surprises. Just mention it when you enquire.

03What does bridal makeup cost?

Bridal makeup starts at ₹10,000 and goes up to ₹28,000 for airbrush, depending on the products you choose. Complete bridal packages, covering skin, wax, hair and makeup, run from ₹15,000 to ₹40,000. The full price list is on the Services page, and I am happy to shape a package around your budget.

04What is the advance payment policy?

An advance is required to confirm your date. I will share the full details once we discuss your package over WhatsApp, so there is nothing hidden and nothing awkward later.

05Can family or bridesmaids be done too?

Of course. Family and bridesmaid add-ons are available with every package. Tell me the headcount when you enquire and I will plan the studio schedule around all of you.

06Which looks do you offer?

Traditional, HD and Airbrush. We choose together at the trial, based on your skin, your outfit and the lighting at your venue. You do not need to know the difference beforehand, that is my job.

07Do you travel to venues?

All work happens in the studio, by design. Controlled lighting is what makes the finish truly camera-ready, and that is hard to promise in a hotel room. If your schedule is tight, message me and we will plan the timings together.

08How long does the bridal session take?

Plan for around two and a half to three hours for the wedding day look, and less for the other functions. You will get exact timings when we plan your schedule, so nobody is ever rushed.
